About Me

Hello, I am WeiJie Liu, a researcher passionate about mathematics, algorithms, and deep learning. I am driven to tackle novel, challenging problems and to advance artificial intelligence.

Since 2024, I have been pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Soochow University. My work explores how models can transform structured data into fluent text and how generated text can be evaluated more reliably.

Research

My research centers on table-to-text generation, text quality evaluation, and interpretable learning systems. I am especially interested in methods that improve models' understanding of structured data while keeping their behavior clear and measurable.

  • Table-to-Text Generation Turning structured records and tables into accurate, readable language.
  • Text Quality Evaluation Measuring factuality, fluency, coverage, and usefulness in generated text.
  • Deep Learning Developing robust, interpretable models for intelligent systems.
